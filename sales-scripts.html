Hi, May I know how to address you? — Miles from NABAFOOD (LA). We keep **LA stock** (MOQ $1000 in CA). May I send our catalog and pricing? Which city/state are you based in?

I am sorry, we supply **ready stock from LA warehouse only**, **no customization**. Inventory: Soup bowls, MFPP clamshells, Gloves, Chopsticks. (Take-out containers: Preorder, 45-day lead to CA/TX). Which city/state are you based in? I can send our catalog and pricing.

Hi! I’ve attached our catalog and price list. **Pricing: min 1 pallet/SKU, whole-pallet only.** We supply **LA stock only, no customization**. If email is more convenient, what’s the best address?
